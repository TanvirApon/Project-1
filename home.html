Shanta started its journey in 1988 in the ready-made garment (RMG) sector and became one of the forerunners in RMG export by establishing leading industries such as Shanta Garments Ltd, Shanta Industries Ltd, Shanta Washworks Ltd, GDS Chemicals Ltd and Shanta Denims Ltd. Earning a solid reputation as an important vendor for some of the most renowned apparel brands of USA and Europe.Shanta Group is also one of the key members of the STS Group-the promoter of the world class Apollo Hospitals Dhaka, International School Dhaka (ISD) the only International Baccalaureate (IB) School of the country,and the Delhi Public School, Dhaka.
